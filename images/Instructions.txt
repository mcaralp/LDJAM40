Your are an adventurer. Always in quest for some valuable treasures, your heard of this temple holding mystic orbs.

The goal is to collect every orbs in the level and put them in your treasure chest. But things aren't that easy: Collect an orb and water will slowly submerge the temple. Collect two orbs or more and the water will flows more and more quickly.

If you feel like running out of time, you can temporarily disable the deadly traps by putting back orbs on the altairs.

Good luck !
